What is Cloud Computing?

Popular internet services like social networks have become more integrated into our daily lives. This, joined with the improving accessibility to high-speed connectivity around the globe has pushed the industry towards server-side computing.

Cloud computing is the delivery of services over the internet on a pay-as-you-go basis. The concept also incorporates the hardware system/software implemented in datacenters. Cloud computing has introduced a new category of computing systems referred to as Warehouse-Scale Computers(WHCs). The term is derived from the fact that these machines have huge software infrastructures, data stores and hardware platforms.
